📖 Biblical Foundation

This assessment is based on the New Testament plan of salvation as practiced in Churches of Christ. According to Scripture, salvation requires:

  • Hearing the Gospel (Romans 10:17)
  • Believing in Jesus Christ (Mark 16:16)
  • Repenting of sins (Acts 2:38)
  • Confessing Jesus as Lord (Romans 10:9-10)
  • Being baptized by immersion for forgiveness (Acts 2:38, Mark 16:16)
  • Living faithfully as a Christian (Revelation 2:10)

🧠 Mental Wellness Activities

🫁 Biblical Breathing Exercise (4-6 Technique)

📋 Instructions:

  1. Inhale through your nose for 4 seconds, breathing deeply into your diaphragm
  2. Exhale through your mouth for 6 seconds, pursing your lips as if breathing through a straw
  3. Focus on God's peace entering as you breathe in, anxiety leaving as you breathe out
  4. Repeat this cycle while meditating on Philippians 4:6-7
