🧘 Breathe from the Heart

A simple breathing exercise to find peace

❤️

Instructions:

  1. Place your hand on your heart
  2. Breathe in slowly through your nose for 4 seconds
  3. Breathe out slowly through your mouth for 6 seconds
  4. Repeat 5 times
  5. Focus on gratitude, care, or compassion

📖 Daily Devotion

5-Step spiritual growth for today

Step 1: Today's Verse

"And we know that in all things God works for the good of those who love him, who have been called according to his purpose." - Romans 8:28

Step 2: Practical Application

When facing challenges today, remember that God is working behind the scenes. Trust His plan even when you can't see the outcome.

Step 3: Reflection Question

How can you trust God's goodness in a current difficult situation?

Step 4: Inspiring Quote

"God's plan for your life is always better than your own." - Unknown

Step 5: Prayer

Lord, help me trust Your perfect plan today. When I'm anxious or fearful, remind me that You work all things for my good. Amen.
